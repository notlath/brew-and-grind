Humble Beginnings

Brew & Grind opened its doors in 2014 at the vibrant corner of P. Noval and Dapitan St. in Sampaloc, Manila. What began as a simple dream—to serve highquality coffee in a warm, welcoming space—has grown into a beloved neighborhood staple.

Where Coffee Meets Community

Brew & Grind opened its doors in 2014 at the vibrant corner of P. Noval and Dapitan St. in Sampaloc, Manila. What began as a simple dream—to serve highquality coffee in a warm, welcoming space—has grown into a beloved neighborhood staple.
